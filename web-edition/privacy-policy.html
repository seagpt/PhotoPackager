Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Effective Date: January 2025

🔒 Your Photos NEVER Leave Your Device

IMPORTANT: PhotoPackager processes all your photos locally on your device. Your photos NEVER leave your device, are NEVER uploaded to our servers, and we NEVER see, store, access, or have any contact with your photos.

100% Client-Side Processing: All photo processing happens entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No photos are ever transmitted over the internet.

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

❌ We NEVER Collect or Access:

  • Your Photos or Images: Photos NEVER leave your device - all processing is local
  • Photo Content: We cannot see, access, or analyze the content of your photos
  • Photo Metadata: EXIF data, location info, and other metadata stays on your device
  • Filenames or Folder Names: Your file organization remains private
  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or contact details required
  • IP Addresses: We don't log or store your IP address
  • Location Data: No geolocation tracking of any kind
  • Device Information: No device fingerprinting
  • Browsing History: No cross-site tracking
  • File System Access: We cannot access other files on your device

3. What Data We May Collect (With Your Consent)

Only if you consent to analytics, we may collect anonymous usage statistics:

  • Usage Metrics: Number of photos processed, processing time
  • Performance Data: Error rates, completion rates
  • Technical Data: Browser type (anonymized), screen resolution
  • Session Data: Anonymous session IDs (not linked to you)

Important: This data is completely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.

4. How We Use Data

Any data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve PhotoPackager's performance and reliability
  • Understand how the service is used to guide development
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Generate aggregate usage statistics

7. Data Security & Photo Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your privacy:

  • 100% Client-Side Processing: Photos NEVER leave your device or browser
  • No Photo Upload: PhotoPackager cannot and does not upload photos to any server
  • Browser Sandboxing: All processing occurs in your browser's secure sandbox
  • HTTPS Encryption: All communications are encrypted
  • Minimal Data Collection: Less data = less risk
  • No Photo Database: We don't maintain any photo databases
  • No Server-Side Photo Processing: Our servers never handle your photos
  • Memory-Only Processing: Photos exist only in your browser's memory during processing
